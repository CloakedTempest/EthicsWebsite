How does AI fit into all this?

A future of AI

A popular topic of conversation is how AI will affect the future of industry and whether AI taking the jobs of people is a good or bad thing. This is a perfect example of where the ethical line gets blured. Since we cannot know how AI will affect society in the future, how can we know whether to let it progress or to class it as "ethicaly wrong" and prevent its development?
